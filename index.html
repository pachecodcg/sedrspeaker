Dr. Mateus Garcia Rocha The Scholar
University of Florida College of Dentistry · Gainesville, Florida
The Scholar

A scientist at the intersection of materials, light, and artificial intelligence. And, first and foremost, a clinician.

Rocha is a dental materials scientist whose work spans the photochemistry of resin-based composites, the engineering of light-curing systems, and the application of artificial intelligence to dental materials research.

His program has been shaped by collaborations with the most respected names in our field worldwide. Since joining the University of Florida in 2019, he has built a research program that bridges academia and industry, earning support from Colgate-Palmolive, Ivoclar, Komet, FAPESP, and internal UF mechanisms.
